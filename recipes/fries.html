Loaded Fries

Description

For your next cheat day, look no further than these loaded fries bursting with flavour.

They're so good, you might just forget you're actually eating a vegetable (potatoes totally count, right?).

Ingredients - Serves 4 as a Side

  • 450g french fries
  • 180g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1 spring onion, sliced
  • 4 rashers smoked bacon, diced
  • 2 jalapenos, sliced

Method

  1. Add the fries to a rimmed baking sheet and cook according to package instructions or until extra crisp, stirring after 10 minutes.
    Alternatively, you can cook the fries in the air fryer and transfer the cooked fries to a baking sheet after they are cooked and crisp.

  2. Top the fries with shredded cheese and cooked crumbled bacon.
    Place the fries back into the oven for 1-3 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

  3. Top with spring onions and sliced jalapenos.
    Serve with your favourite sauce.
