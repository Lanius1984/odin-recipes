Saag Aloo

Description

Saag Aloo features tender & flavorsome potatoes, sauteed with spices, spinach or your favorite greens.

Make this one pot meal under 45 minutes on the stovetop or in the Instant pot with my easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - I use white potatoes and do not recommend new potatoes as they can get too mushy and break down.
  • Spinach - Use young or baby greens. If you are using mature greens, trim down the stalks as they can taste bitter.
  • Tomatoes - I use pureed fresh tomatoes. Remember saag does not need lots of tomatoes.
