Saag Aloo

Description

Saag Aloo features tender & flavorsome potatoes, sauteed with spices, and finished with spinach.

Make this meal in under 30 minutes with this easy to follow recipe.

Ingredients - Serves 4 as a Side

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, chopped
  • 500g potato, diced
  • 1 red chilli, de-seeded and sliced
  • 0.5 tsp each - black mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric, salt
  • 250g spinach leaves

Method

  1. Heat the sunflower oil in a large pan, add the onion, garlic cloves and chopped ginger.
    Fry for about 3 mins.
  2. Stir in the potatoes, red chilli, black mustard seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric and salt.
    Continue cooking and stirring for 5 mins more.
  3. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook for 8-10 mins.
  4. Check the potatoes are ready by spearing with the point of a knife, and if they are, add the spinach leaves and let it wilt into the pan.
    Take off the heat and serve.
